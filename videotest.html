Using ffmpeg-asm.js...

  1. WebM can be converted in mp4.
  2. WAV can be converted in ogg or mp3 (AAC/Vorbis).
  3. WAV can be merged in mp4.

If WAV or WebM is 5MB in size; ogg, mp3 or mp4 will be 700kb!
